About the Data

Where does the data come from?

The majority of the data found on this app is downloaded from the Texas Education Agency’s annual Texas Academic Performance Report.

The TEA provides master reference tables where the raw data can be referenced against keys that we use to label our sections.

We also used TEA’s shape data to map districts and campuses which can be found here. Note that the district shapefiles are approximate and should be used for general information, not for determining district affiliation.

Superintendents, principals, and campus and district contact information are pulled from AskTED.
